About Us

Our Mission

Our practice is committed to deliver personalized care to help improve our patient’s quality of life. We strive to provide a high-quality patient care and we believe in working with our patients to maintain and improve their health and assist patients with different pain treatment options. Out vision is to provide the best Orthopedic and Pain Management care, along with education and research.

Miami Gardens Orthopedics and Pain Management

Miami Gardens Orthopedics and Pain Management is a state-of-the-art, highly specialized Orthopedics and Pain Management facility dedicated to treating the full spectrum of pain, from common disorders such as neck pain, upper and lower back pain, headache, migraines, sciatica, hand and arm pain to other less common painful conditions through a wide rage of non-surgical procedures and techniques. Our goal is to improve your quality of life so that you can return to a more functional pain-free lifestyle.

Our board-certified Orthopedics and Pain Management physicians will help you manage your condition in order to achieve pain relief. At Miami Gardens Orthopedics and Pain Management, we have the expertise, the experience, and the technology to deliver excellence in orthopedic care, specialized to meet your individual needs. we are committed to providing tailored plans of action for each patient. Whether you suffered an injury from an accident or whether you’re suffering from sciatica, chronic neck or back pain, our board-certified orthopedics, pain management physicians and skilled staff are here to help.

Treating Pain from Head to Toe

We are proud to say that all of our doctors employ the latest and most advanced treatment options available,
offering our patients a cutting-edge and modern approach to pain relief and non-invasive treatments. Below
you will find examples of some of the most common areas of the body that can experience unexplained pain:

Hip, Leg and Pelvis

Because the hip joint, lower back, and leg bones are all connected, hip and leg pain can have a wide range of causes. Whether you have sacroiliac joint dysfunction, hip bursitis or a common sprain or strain, we are here to help.

Hand, Wrist and Elbow

Pain and injuries to the hand, wrist and elbow can interfere with your daily life and ability to work. Whether you have Tennis elbow, Trigger Finger, Carpal Tunnel or a common sprain or strain, we are here to help.

Back and Neck

Pain and injuries to the back, neck and spine are the most common conditions treated at Miami Gardens Orthopedics and Pain Management whether you have sciatica, a spinal fracture or a common sprain or strain, we are here to help.

Shoulder

Shoulder pain is most often caused by an injury to the rotator cuff, there are alternatives causes related to arthritis, cartilage damage and other injuries. Whether you have a rotator cuff injury, frozen shoulder or shoulder impingement, we are here to help.

Knee

Knee pain can be caused by overuse, a sudden injury or an underlying condition, Miami Gardens Orthopedics and Pain Management team specializes in knee pain, including treatment for knee bursitis and sprain and strains of the ACL, LCL and MCL.

Foot and Ankle

Pain and injuries to the foot and ankle can keep you from getting around, from working and playing, whether you have Plantar Fasciitis, Achilles Tendinitis or a common sprain or strain, we are here to help.
